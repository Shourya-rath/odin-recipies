Description

Paneer Butter Masala is a rich, creamy, and mildly spiced North Indian curry made with paneer (Indian cottage cheese), tomatoes, butter, and a blend of aromatic spices. The dish is known for its velvety smooth texture, thanks to a luscious tomato-based gravy enriched with cashews and cream. The butter enhances the flavors, making it a popular choice in Indian restaurants and a favorite for special occasions

This dish pairs exceptionally well with naan, roti, or jeera rice. The slight sweetness of the tomato gravy, combined with the mild spices and soft paneer cubes, creates a perfectly balanced dish. It is easy to prepare at home with simple ingredients and delivers restaurant-style taste in just a few steps.

Ingredients

  • 200g paneer, cubed
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 2 tbsp cashew paste
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • ½ tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • ¼ cup fresh cream

Steps

  1. Heat butter in a pan and add cumin seeds.
  2. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Stir in ginger-garlic paste and cook until fragrant.
  4. Pour in tomato puree and cook until the oil separates.

  7. Gently add paneer cubes and let them cook for 2 minutes.
  8. Stir in fresh cream, mix well, and turn off the heat.
