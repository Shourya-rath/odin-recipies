Description

Masala Aloo is a flavorful and comforting Indian dish made with potatoes, aromatic spices, and herbs. This dish is aeasy to prepare and pairs well with roti, rice, or even as a filling for wraps. The potatoes are boiled and then sautéed with a blend of spices like cumin, turmeric, and red chili powder, giving them a deliciously spiced and slightly crispy texture. Fresh coriander and lemon juice add a refreshing touch, balancing the bold flavors of the spices.

This dish is perfect for a quick meal or a side dish at any gathering. You can customize it by adding green chilies for extra heat or mixing in some peas for a variation. Whether served with dal-rice or stuffed into a paratha, masala aloo is a satisfying and versatile dish that never fails to impress.

Ingredients

  • 3 medium potatoes, boiled and cubed
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • ½ tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • ½ tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp chopped coriander leaves
  • 1 tsp lemon juice

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds, letting them splutter.
  2. Add the boiled and cubed potatoes and sauté for a minute.
  3. Sprinkle turmeric, red chili powder, coriander powder, and salt, then mix well.
  4. Cook for 5-7 minutes on medium heat, stirring occasionally.
  5. Add garam masala and mix well.
  6. Turn off the heat and add chopped coriander leaves and lemon juice.
  7. Serve hot with roti, paratha, or rice
